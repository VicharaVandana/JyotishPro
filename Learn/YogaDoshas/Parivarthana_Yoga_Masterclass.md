# Parivarthana Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Parivarthana Yoga** (Exchange of Houses) is a powerful Nabhasa Yoga formed when two planets reside in each other's signs. It creates a deep, unbreakable link between the two houses and their significations.

## 2. Formation Rules
Planet A is in Planet B's sign, AND Planet B is in Planet A's sign. 
(e.g., Mars in Cancer, Moon in Aries).

## 3. Types of Parivarthana
1. **Maha Yoga:** Exchange between Lords of auspicious houses (Kendras/Trikonas/2nd/11th). Brings wealth, status, and great fortune.
2. **Khala Yoga:** Exchange involving the 3rd Lord. Brings fluctuations, where the native rises through immense self-effort but faces recurring instability.
3. **Dainya Yoga:** Exchange involving Dusthanas (6th, 8th, or 12th Lords). Creates struggles, enmity, debts, or health issues, but can also form Vipareeta Raja Yoga if both lords are from Dusthanas.

## 4. Results
The two houses involved effectively act as one unit. The planets empower each other, functioning almost as if they were placed in their own signs, deeply entwining the destinies of the associated house matters.
