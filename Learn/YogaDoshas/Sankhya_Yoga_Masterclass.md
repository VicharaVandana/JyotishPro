# Sankhya Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Sankhya Yogas** are Nabhasa Yogas based entirely on the **number (Sankhya)** of signs occupied by the seven physical planets (excluding Rahu and Ketu).

## 2. Formation & Types
Depending on how many signs the 7 planets are distributed across:
1. **Gola (1 sign):** Destitute, lazy, or a profound ascetic.
2. **Yuga (2 signs):** Poor, heretical, or highly focused on specific goals.
3. **Shoola (3 signs):** Brave, argumentative, sharp, often carries physical marks or injuries.
4. **Kedara (4 signs):** Agricultural wealth, truthful, helpful to many.
5. **Pasha (5 signs):** Large family, wealthy, but bound by many worldly attachments.
6. **Daamini (6 signs):** Wealthy, famous, helps others, highly liberal.
7. **Veena / Vallaki (7 signs):** Lover of arts, music, highly cultured, enjoys widespread prosperity.

## 3. Significance
The more spread out the planets (higher number of signs), the more balanced and worldly successful the native tends to be. Tightly clustered planets create extreme, highly concentrated energy.
