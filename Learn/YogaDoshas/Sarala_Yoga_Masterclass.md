# Masterclass: Sarala Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Sarala Yoga is a Vipareeta Raja Yoga formed by the 8th Lord [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: The Lord of the 8th house must be placed in the 6th, 8th, or 12th house [cite: BPHS, Phaladeepika].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native is long-lived, courageous, and prosperous [cite: BPHS].
- Provides the ability to handle sudden transformations and crises with ease [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Gains through research, hidden knowledge, or overcoming unexpected challenges [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Heavy malefic influence (Papa Kartari) on the 8th Lord.
- Severe debilitation without Neecha Bhanga.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: If the 8th Lord is in its own sign or exalted while in a Dusthana.
- Weakening: Combustion or being caught in a planetary war (Graha Yuddha) where the 8th Lord is defeated.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a beneficial yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- The yoga brings success through the mastery of the eighth house significations: occult, sudden change, and longevity.
