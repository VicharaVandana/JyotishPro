# Aakriti Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Aakriti Yogas** form part of the Nabhasa Yogas. "Aakriti" means shape or figure. These yogas are determined by the geometric pattern the seven planets form in the birth chart.

## 2. Formation & Types (Examples)
There are 20 Aakriti Yogas. Some notable ones include:
- **Gada (Mace):** All planets in two adjacent Kendras. Denotes wealth but a commanding, sometimes aggressive nature.
- **Shakata (Cart):** All planets in the 1st and 7th houses. (Distinct from the Moon-Jupiter Sakata Dosha). The native may suffer poverty or a fluctuating life, like a rolling wheel.
- **Vihaga (Bird):** All planets in the 4th and 10th houses. The native travels extensively, acts as a messenger or ambassador.
- **Vajra (Thunderbolt):** Benefics in 1st/7th and Malefics in 4th/10th. Beautiful but fierce, brave, and sometimes turbulent in early life.

## 3. Significance
Aakriti Yogas operate continuously throughout life, unaffected by Dasha periods, forming the immutable background structure of the native's destiny.
