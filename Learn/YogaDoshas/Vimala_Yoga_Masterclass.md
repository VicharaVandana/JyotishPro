# Masterclass: Vimala Yoga (Vipareeta Raja Yoga)

## 1. Scriptural Reference & Formation Rules
**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6, Shloka 63 (or 67 depending on translation):**
> *“Vyayeshe vyayabhava sthite... Vimalayogo bhavati...”*
> **Meaning:** Vimala Yoga is formed when the Lord of the 12th house is placed in the 6th, 8th, or 12th house.

**B.V. Raman's "Three Hundred Important Combinations" (Vipareeta Raja Yogas):**
Dr. Raman explains that the 12th house rules loss, expenditure, hospitalization, and isolation. When the lord of loss is placed in another house of loss (like the 8th or 6th), the concept of "loss" itself is lost. The native thereby accumulates wealth and enjoys a peaceful mind.

## 2. Results & Effects
**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6, Shloka 63:**
> *“The native with Vimala Yoga will spend less and save much. He will be good to everybody, be happy and independent. He will follow a respectable profession, be known for his good qualities, and will possess a pure mind.”*

**Modern Synthesis:**
Vimala Yoga natives are excellent financial planners because the 12th house of expenditure is strictly controlled. They often gain wealth from foreign sources, multinational corporations, or careers that require isolation (like research or coding). They possess a naturally meditative and content disposition.

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- **Association with Wealth Lords:** If the 12th lord conjuncts the 2nd lord (wealth) or 11th lord (gains) in a Dusthana, it cancels the Vipareeta effect and instead causes severe financial losses or bankruptcy. The 12th lord must act alone.
- **Aspect of Malefics:** If heavily aspected by Mars or Saturn, the "pure mind" aspect of the yoga is disturbed, leading to forced isolation rather than chosen independence.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- **Strengthening Factors:** Placement of the 12th lord in the 12th house itself (Swakshetra) is exceptionally powerful for spiritual liberation (Moksha) and accumulating wealth without apparent effort. 
- **Weakening Factors:** If the 12th lord is combust, the native's independence is compromised, and they may be forced to serve others or live in environments they do not like.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
*Not applicable.* The yoga grants a naturally pure mind. Engaging in regular charity (donating a small portion of savings) ensures the 12th house energy is satisfied positively, preventing unexpected medical expenditures.

## 6. Notes & Further Details
- **B.V. Raman's Observation:** "Vimala" translates to pure or stainless. Dr. Raman notes that Vimala Yoga natives rarely engage in office politics or vicious competition. They achieve their high status while remaining entirely independent and untainted by societal corruption.
- **Scriptural Nuance:** The text highlights "independent" (Swatantra). The ultimate blessing of Vimala Yoga is not just wealth, but freedom—freedom from debt, freedom from oppressive bosses, and freedom from psychological anxieties.
