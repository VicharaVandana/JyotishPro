# Masterclass: Parvata Yoga

## 1. Scriptural Reference & Formation Rules
**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6 (Yogas), Shloka 15:**
> *“Kendre saumyagrahairukte...”*
> **Meaning:** Parvata Yoga is formed when benefic planets occupy the Kendras (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th houses), and the 6th and 8th houses are either completely vacant or occupied exclusively by benefic planets. (Malefics must not occupy the 6th or 8th).

**B.V. Raman's "Three Hundred Important Combinations" (Combination No. 43):**
Dr. Raman outlines this exact definition, emphasizing that Kendras represent the pillars of life. When these are fortified by benefics, and the houses of disease/debt (6th) and sudden crises/death (8th) are devoid of malefic influence, the native's life becomes as stable and majestic as a mountain.

## 2. Results & Effects
**Phaladeepika & BPHS:**
> *“The person born with Parvata Yoga will have lasting wealth and prosperity. They will be charitable, highly learned, very passionate, and will be a leader or head of a town, city, or organization.”*

**Modern Synthesis & B.V. Raman:**
Natives with Parvata Yoga exhibit immense stability in their careers and personal lives. They are not easily shaken by crises because the 8th house is protected. They possess an aura of authority and calm, often becoming mayors, CEOs, deans of universities, or spiritual leaders. Their success is enduring and robust, rarely suffering sudden downfalls.

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- **Malefic Intrusion:** The most critical breaking point for this yoga is the placement of a malefic (Saturn, Mars, Rahu, Ketu, Sun) in the 6th or 8th house. This completely shatters the "Parvata" (Mountain) effect, introducing cracks (enemies, diseases, crises) into the foundation of the native's life.
- **Affliction of Kendras:** If the benefics in the Kendras are heavily conjunct with malefics, the purity of the yoga is ruined, reducing the native's fame to notoriety or causing their wealth to be unstable.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- **Strengthening Factors:** The yoga is exceptionally powerful if the benefics in the Kendras are in their own or exaltation signs (e.g., forming Hamsa, Malavya, or Bhadra Yogas simultaneously). This grants the native almost legendary status and unshakeable prosperity.
- **Weakening Factors:** If the benefics forming the yoga are debilitated or deeply combust, the native has the *desire* for stability and leadership, but struggles to achieve it due to a lack of inner vitality or resources.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
*Not applicable.* This is a highly auspicious structural yoga that grants enduring protection. However, the native should avoid arrogance, as a "Mountain" can still be eroded if they neglect their charitable duties.

## 6. Notes & Further Details
- **B.V. Raman's Observation:** Dr. Raman stresses that "Parvata" literally translates to Mountain. The key characteristic of this yoga is *permanence*. Unlike some Raja Yogas that give sudden but brief periods of extreme wealth, Parvata Yoga gives steady, continuous growth that lasts a lifetime.
- **Scriptural Nuance:** The strict condition that the 6th and 8th must be devoid of malefics is profound. It implies that true, unshakeable success (Mountain) is only possible when a person is free from the hidden enemies of debt, disease, and secret vices.
