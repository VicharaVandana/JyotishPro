# Masterclass: Hamsa Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Hamsa Yoga is a Mahapurusha Yoga formed by Jupiter [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: Jupiter must be in its own sign (Sagittarius/Pisces) or its exaltation sign (Cancer) AND must occupy a Kendra house [cite: BPHS].

## 2. Results & Effects
- Native is noble, religious, and highly virtuous [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Possesses great wisdom and attracts respect from high-ranking individuals or the state [cite: BPHS].
- Healthy, wealthy, and enjoys long life [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta): Jupiter being too close to the Sun.
- Severe affliction by Rahu (Guru-Chandala Dosha) without strong benefic protection.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Placement in the 1st house (Lagna).
- Weakening: If Jupiter is retrograde (though debated, some texts say it reduces the immediate Mahapurusha effect) or heavily afflicted.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a highly auspicious Mahapurusha Yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- Indicates deep spiritual inclination and ethical living.
