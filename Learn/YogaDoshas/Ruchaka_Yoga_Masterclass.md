# Masterclass: Ruchaka Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Ruchaka Yoga is one of the five Mahapurusha Yogas [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: Mars must be in its own sign (Aries/Scorpio) or its exaltation sign (Capricorn) AND must occupy a Kendra (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th house) [cite: BPHS, Phaladeepika].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native is brave, powerful, handsome, and possessive of great vitality [cite: BPHS].
- Successful in military, sports, or leadership roles due to immense courage and competitive spirit [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Gains fame, wealth, and command over others [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta): Mars being combust by the Sun.
- Severe Affliction: Being hemmed between natural malefics (Papa Kartari) without benefic aspect.
- Defeat in Graha Yuddha (Planetary War).

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Placement in the 10th house (Digbala) or conjunct/aspected by Jupiter.
- Weakening: Placement in the 6th, 8th, or 12th houses relative to the Lagna (if not in a Kendra) or severe affliction by Rahu/Saturn.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a highly auspicious Mahapurusha Yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- The yoga brings success specifically through assertive and proactive initiatives. Its results are most pronounced during Mars Dasha.
