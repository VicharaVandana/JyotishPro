# Sreenatha Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Sreenatha Yoga** is a highly specific and rare Raja Yoga that bestows wealth, power, and a lifestyle comparable to royalty or divine figures (Sreenatha refers to Lord Vishnu).

## 2. Formation Rules
According to classical texts, this Yoga is formed when:
- The **Lord of the 7th house is exalted in the 10th house**, AND
- The **Lord of the 9th house is also associated with the 10th house** (either by conjunction with the 10th lord or placement in the 10th).

## 3. Results & Effects
- Grants immense wealth, fame, and a highly charismatic public persona.
- The native's spouse (7th house) often plays a significant role in their professional rise (10th house) and fortune (9th house).
- Denotes a highly moral and fortunate individual who commands respect effortlessly.

## 4. Rarity
Because it requires specific ascendants where the 7th lord can be exalted in the 10th (e.g., Sagittarius Ascendant where Mercury is 7th lord exalted in Virgo in the 10th), it is a very rare combination.
