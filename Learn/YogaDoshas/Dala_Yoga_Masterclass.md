# Dala Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Dala Yogas** are a subset of Nabhasa Yogas that focus exclusively on the placement of natural Benefics and natural Malefics in the Angles (Kendras).

## 2. Formation & Types
1. **Mala / Sruk Yoga:** Formed when all three Kendras (apart from the Ascendant) are occupied entirely by **natural benefic planets** (Jupiter, Venus, well-associated Mercury).
   - *Result:* The native enjoys a comfortable life, is adorned with wealth, has a gentle disposition, and is loved by the masses.
2. **Sarpa Yoga:** Formed when the Kendras are occupied entirely by **natural malefic planets** (Sun, Mars, Saturn).
   - *Result:* The native faces constant struggles, harsh realities, and may possess a cruel or excessively stern demeanor. Life is characterized by intense battles.

## 3. Nuances
Dala Yogas show what energies are driving the foundation (Kendras) of the chart. Benefics bring grace; Malefics bring friction and necessity for sheer force.
