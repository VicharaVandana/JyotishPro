# Masterclass: Sreenatha Yoga

## 1. Scriptural Reference & Formation Rules
**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6 (Yogas), Shloka 28:**
> *“Dharmakarmadhipau... saptameshe karmasthe swaochhe Sreenathayogah...”*
> **Meaning:** Sreenatha Yoga is formed under very specific conditions:
> 1. The Lord of the 7th house must be placed in the 10th house in a state of exaltation.
> 2. The Lord of the 10th house must be conjunct the Lord of the 9th house.

**B.V. Raman's "Three Hundred Important Combinations" (Combination No. 123):**
Dr. Raman highlights that this is a highly specific and rare Raja Yoga. Because it requires the 7th lord to be exalted in the 10th, it can only technically occur for a few Ascendants (Lagnas), most notably Sagittarius (where Mercury, the 7th lord, is exalted in Virgo, the 10th house). 

## 2. Results & Effects
**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6, Shloka 28:**
> *“The person born with Sreenatha Yoga will have the marks of the conch, wheel, and lotus (symbols of Lord Vishnu) on his body. He will be a king of kings, speak softly and sweetly, possess a beautiful and attractive body, and will be endowed with a good wife and children. He will be deeply devoted to Lord Narayana (Vishnu).”*

**Modern Synthesis:**
The native attains an almost mythological level of success, charm, and divine grace. The involvement of the 7th lord (partnerships/spouse) and 10th lord (career) indicates that their massive professional success is intrinsically tied to their marriage or public partnerships.

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- **Affliction to Mercury (for Sagittarius Lagna):** If Mercury (the 7th lord exalted in the 10th) is deeply combust by the Sun or heavily afflicted by Saturn/Mars, the "sweet speech" and diplomatic grace of the yoga are ruined, leading to public scandals instead of reverence.
- **Affliction to the 9th/10th Conjunction:** If the 9th and 10th lords (the Sun and Mercury, in the case of Sagittarius Lagna) are placed in the 6th, 8th, or 12th house, the yoga is fractured, reducing the "king of kings" status to mere upper-middle-class prosperity.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- **Strengthening Factors:** If Jupiter (the Lagna lord for Sagittarius) aspects the 10th house or the conjunction of the 9th/10th lords, it seals the yoga with absolute divine protection and deep spiritual wisdom.
- **Weakening Factors:** If the native abandons ethical conduct or engages in deceit (violating the traits of Lord Vishnu), the yoga ceases to function at its highest octave.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
*Not applicable.* However, classical texts strongly imply that maintaining devotion to Lord Vishnu (or living by the principles of preservation, truth, and duty) is necessary to unlock the full potential of this yoga.

## 6. Notes & Further Details
- **B.V. Raman's Observation:** Dr. Raman notes the rarity of this yoga. It perfectly synthesizes the 7th (public relations/spouse), the 9th (fortune/grace), and the 10th (career/power). It creates an individual whose public and private lives are flawlessly aligned and universally admired.
- **Scriptural Nuance:** "Sreenatha" translates to "Lord of Fortune/Wealth," an epithet for Lord Vishnu. The specific physical marks (conch, lotus) mentioned in Phaladeepika are poetic metaphors for a native who possesses an aura of divine authority, peace, and unshakeable prosperity.
