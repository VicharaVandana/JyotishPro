# Masterclass: Hamsa Yoga (A Pancha Mahapurusha Yoga)

## 1. Scriptural Reference & Formation Rules
**Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS) - Chapter 77, Shloka 3:**
> *“Swaochhaswasthangate Jeeve Kantake Hamsasanjnakah...”*
> **Meaning:** When Jupiter (Jeeva) is situated in its exaltation sign (Cancer) or its own signs (Sagittarius or Pisces), and simultaneously occupies a Kendra (Angular house: 1st, 4th, 7th, or 10th), the resulting combination is called Hamsa Yoga.

**B.V. Raman's "Three Hundred Important Combinations" (Combination No. 70 - Panchamahapurusha Yogas):**
Dr. Raman defines Hamsa Yoga identically and emphasizes that the true strength of this yoga relies heavily on the overall dignity of Jupiter and its freedom from malefic afflictions.

## 2. Results & Effects
**Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS):**
BPHS states that one born in Hamsa Yoga will have a voice like a swan, a handsome physical appearance, will be a king or equal to a king, and will be endowed with many virtues, enjoying a long life of 100 years.

**Phaladeepika - Chapter 6, Shloka 4:**
> *“The person born in Hamsa Yoga will be a king whose feet will be praised by other kings. He will bear auspicious marks like the conch, lotus, and fish on his hands and feet. He possesses a handsome body, enjoys pure, sweet food, and is inherently righteous.”*

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
To manifest as a true Mahapurusha (Great Personality) yoga, Jupiter must be pure. The yoga suffers a Bhanga (cancellation or severe reduction) under these conditions:
- **Combustion (Asta):** If Jupiter is too close to the Sun (combust), the spiritual and ethical light is burned out by ego.
- **Guru-Chandala Dosha:** Severe affliction by Rahu (conjunction) without any strong benefic aspect pollutes Jupiter's wisdom with obsessive or unethical tendencies, cancelling the Hamsa effect.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- **Strengthening Factors:** Placement in the Lagna (1st house) maximizes its impact on the native's core personality. Aspect by a well-placed Moon (forming Gaja Kesari simultaneously) amplifies the Hamsa Yoga exponentially.
- **Weakening Factors:** If Jupiter is retrograde (Vakri), classical texts suggest the outward manifestation of the Mahapurusha results may be delayed or internalized. Placement in the Navamsa (D9) in a debilitated sign (Capricorn) severely weakens the yoga.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
*Not applicable.* Hamsa Yoga is a supremely auspicious combination. However, to maximize its potential, the native is advised to maintain a strict adherence to truth (Satya) and respect for teachers and gurus.

## 6. Notes & Further Details
- **B.V. Raman's Observation:** Dr. Raman emphasizes that Hamsa Yoga makes a person pious, respected by the masses, and highly righteous. Their words carry immense weight and are naturally obeyed by others.
- **Scriptural Nuance:** The term "Hamsa" refers to the mythical Swan capable of separating milk from water. Astrologically, this denotes the native's supreme discriminative intellect (Viveka) — the ability to distinguish truth from illusion.
