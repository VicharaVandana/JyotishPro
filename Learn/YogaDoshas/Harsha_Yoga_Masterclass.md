# Masterclass: Harsha Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Harsha Yoga is a specific type of Vipareeta Raja Yoga [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: The Lord of the 6th house must be placed in the 6th, 8th, or 12th house [cite: BPHS, Phaladeepika].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native gains victory over enemies, possesses good health, and enjoys a high level of happiness [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Grants the ability to turn difficult situations into advantageous ones [cite: BPHS].
- Success in overcoming debts and legal disputes [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Association with Malefics: If the planet forming the yoga is heavily afflicted by natural malefics without benefic aspect.
- Placement: If the planet, while in a Dusthana, is also debilitated.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Placement in the 6th house (own house) or if aspected by natural benefics.
- Weakening: Combustion (Asta) by the Sun or severe affliction by Rahu/Ketu.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a beneficial yoga formed through the transmutation of negative houses.

## 6. Technical Notes
- Vipareeta Raja Yogas function through the principle of "loss of loss" or "gain from adversity." The strength depends on the planet not being otherwise afflicted in the chart.
