# Masterclass: Mixed Voshi Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- **The Rule:** Formed when both benefics and malefics occupy the 12th house from the natal Sun.

## 2. Results & Effects
This creates a deeply divided subconscious foundation.
- **The Internal Battle:** The native will possess strong spiritual, philosophical, and charitable inclinations (driven by the benefics). However, they will simultaneously battle hidden fears, vindictive thoughts, or periods of severe vitality drain (driven by the malefics).
- **Karmic Push-and-Pull:** Their life is often characterized by a struggle to choose the "high road" of forgiveness and spirituality over the "low road" of fear, revenge, or isolation.

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- **Combustion of the Benefic:** If the benefic planet is combust by the Sun but the malefic is not, the spiritual protection is removed, leaving the native's subconscious highly vulnerable to anxiety and malefic energy.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- **Strengthening Factors:** A strong, dignified benefic (like an exalted Venus or Jupiter) overpowering a weak malefic leads to massive spiritual triumphs over the native's inner demons.
- **Weakening Factors:** If the malefic wins a planetary war (Graha Yuddha) against the benefic, the native's fears and baser instincts often sabotage their spiritual growth.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Seva (selfless service) and anonymous donations to charity to activate the benefic 12th house energies and consciously suppress the malefic ones.

## 6. Technical Notes
This yoga reflects a profound karmic testing ground where the native must consciously and repeatedly choose higher philosophical paths over their baser, fearful subconscious reactions.
