# Aashraya Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Aashraya Yogas** are a subset of Nabhasa Yogas based entirely on the nature of the signs (Movable, Fixed, or Dual) occupied by all the seven physical planets.

## 2. Formation & Types
1. **Rajju Yoga:** All planets are in **Movable (Chara) signs** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn).
   - *Result:* The native is highly ambitious, loves travel, constantly seeks change, and achieves fortune in foreign lands.
2. **Musala Yoga:** All planets are in **Fixed (Sthira) signs** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius).
   - *Result:* The native is steadfast, resolute, accumulates wealth slowly but steadily, and enjoys a stable, secure life.
3. **Nala Yoga:** All planets are in **Dual (Dwisvabhava) signs** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces).
   - *Result:* The native is adaptable, intelligent, possesses a dual nature, and often pursues multiple careers or interests simultaneously.

## 3. Significance
These yogas define the core modus operandi of the individual—whether they are initiators (Movable), sustainers (Fixed), or adapters (Dual).
