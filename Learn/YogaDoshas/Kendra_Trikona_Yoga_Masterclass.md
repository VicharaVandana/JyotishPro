# Kendra Trikona Yoga Masterclass

## 1. Introduction
**Kendra Trikona Yoga** is the fundamental building block of most Raja Yogas (combinations for royalty and success) in Vedic Astrology. 

## 2. Formation Rules
This Yoga occurs when there is a relationship (conjunction, mutual aspect, or exchange) between a **Kendra Lord (1st, 4th, 7th, or 10th house)** and a **Trikona Lord (1st, 5th, or 9th house)**.
*Note: The Ascendant (1st house) is uniquely both a Kendra and a Trikona.*

## 3. Results & Effects
- **Kendras** represent action, foundation, and the physical pillars of life (Vishnu Sthanas).
- **Trikonas** represent intelligence, past life merit, and fortune (Lakshmi Sthanas).
When they combine, effort (Kendra) meets luck (Trikona):
- The native achieves success, prosperity, and authority.
- Efforts yield highly favorable results.
- Promotes overall harmony between one's professional life and personal fortune.

## 4. Nuances
- The yoga is most powerful when it involves the 9th and 10th lords (Dharma-Karmadhipati Yoga).
- If the lords involved also own malefic houses (like the 8th or 11th), the yoga's purity is slightly tainted, bringing success alongside some struggle.
