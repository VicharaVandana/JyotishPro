# Masterclass: Anapha Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Anapha Yoga is formed under the following specific planetary conditions [cite: 54]:
- One or more planets must occupy the 12th house from the natal Moon [cite: 54].
- The Sun, Rahu, and Ketu are strictly excluded from forming this yoga [cite: 55].
- Only Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, and Saturn can construct a valid Anapha Yoga [cite: 56].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The 12th house from the Moon represents subconscious expenditures, physical comforts, and spiritual inclinations [cite: 57].
- Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS): States the native will be a king (or equivalent in modern terms), strong, healthy, well-mannered, and famous [cite: 58].
- Phaladeepika: Adds that the native will possess great self-respect, enjoy sensual and material comforts, but maintain a generous and unattached disposition [cite: 59].
- Material vs. Spiritual: Benefics here grant peaceful sleep, spiritual wealth, and charitable tendencies, while malefics may cause expenses related to health or disputes [cite: 60].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta) by the Sun renders the yoga-forming planet powerless [cite: 62].
- The planet suffering severe defeat in a planetary war (Graha Yuddha) [cite: 63].
- The planet being deeply debilitated (Neecha) without any Neecha Bhanga (cancellation of debilitation) [cite: 64].

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening Factors: The planet forming the yoga being in exaltation, Moolatrikona, Vargottama, or its own sign [cite: 65].
- Benefics (Jupiter, Venus) produce highly ethical outcomes [cite: 66].
- Weakening Factors: The forming planet being debilitated, retrograde, or hemmed in a Papa Kartari (between two malefics) [cite: 67].

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. Anapha Yoga is an auspicious planetary combination [cite: 68].

## 6. Technical Notes
- The most prominent effects of Anapha Yoga will manifest during the Mahadasha or Antardasha of the planet situated in the 12th house from the Moon [cite: 69].
