# Masterclass: Shasha Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Shasha Yoga is a Mahapurusha Yoga formed by Saturn [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: Saturn must be in its own sign (Capricorn/Aquarius) or its exaltation sign (Libra) AND must occupy a Kendra house [cite: BPHS].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native is a leader, often commanding over people, and possesses great patience [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Indicates high professional status, enduring wealth, and authority [cite: BPHS].
- Can indicate success in politics or government-related work [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta): Saturn being combust by the Sun.
- Severe affliction by Mars (can cause volatile/harsh results) or the Nodes.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Placement in the 7th or 10th house.
- Weakening: If Saturn is debilitated or caught in severe Papa Kartari.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a highly auspicious Mahapurusha Yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- Results often manifest more strongly in the second half of life, requiring discipline and perseverance.
