# Masterclass: Malavya Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Malavya Yoga is a Mahapurusha Yoga formed by Venus [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: Venus must be in its own sign (Taurus/Libra) or its exaltation sign (Pisces) AND must occupy a Kendra house [cite: BPHS].

## 2. Results & Effects
- Native possesses great beauty, charm, and comfort [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Often leads to a luxurious lifestyle, success in arts, and happiness in relationships [cite: BPHS].
- Highly respected in society and possesses wealth [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta): Venus being too close to the Sun.
- Severe affliction by natural malefics like Saturn or Mars without benefic support.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Conjunction with Mercury or Jupiter.
- Weakening: If Venus is placed in an incompatible house or severely afflicted by Rahu.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a highly auspicious Mahapurusha Yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- Enhances artistic talent and material prosperity significantly.
