# Masterclass: Vimala Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Vimala Yoga is a Vipareeta Raja Yoga formed by the 12th Lord [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: The Lord of the 12th house must be placed in the 6th, 8th, or 12th house [cite: BPHS, Phaladeepika].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native is pure in conduct, happy, and independent [cite: BPHS].
- Grants success in managing expenses and attaining spiritual liberation (Moksha) [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Leads to wealth and self-sufficiency, often without relying on others [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Association with natural malefics (like Saturn or Mars) that are not functional benefics.
- Combustion (Asta) by the Sun.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: If the 12th Lord is in its own sign or aspected by benefics like Jupiter or Venus.
- Weakening: If the 12th Lord is debilitated or caught in a Papa Kartari.

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a beneficial yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- The "Vimala" (pure) quality suggests the native uses their resources wisely and ethically, often leading to a life of refined simplicity and spiritual richness.
