# Masterclass: Bhadra Yoga

## 1. Formation Rules (Jyotish Sutras)
- Bhadra Yoga is a Mahapurusha Yoga formed by Mercury [cite: BPHS].
- Formation: Mercury must be in its own sign (Gemini/Virgo) or its exaltation sign (Virgo) AND must occupy a Kendra house [cite: BPHS].

## 2. Results & Effects
- The native is highly intelligent, eloquent, and possessor of sharp analytical skills [cite: Phaladeepika].
- Often leads to high status in business, academics, or communication-related fields [cite: BPHS].
- Wealthy, respected, and long-lived [cite: BPHS].

## 3. Cancellation/Mitigation (Bhanga)
- Combustion (Asta): Mercury being combust by the Sun.
- Severe affliction by harsh malefics like Mars or Saturn without benefic support.

## 4. Factors Modifying Strength
- Strengthening: Conjunction with a benefic (like Venus or Jupiter).
- Weakening: If Mercury is debilitated in a different chart/navamsha or severely afflicted by the Nodes (Rahu/Ketu).

## 5. Prescribed Remedies
- Not applicable. This is a highly auspicious Mahapurusha Yoga.

## 6. Technical Notes
- The native demonstrates superior learning capabilities and communication prowess.
